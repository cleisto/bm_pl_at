Privacy Policy — BTC Power Law Monitor

Last updated: April 17, 2026

1. Overview

BTC Power Law Monitor ("the App") is a Bitcoin analysis tool that displays Power Law model projections. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any data associated with your use of the App.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. The App does not require registration, does not ask for your name, email, or any identifying information, and does not use analytics or tracking services.

3. Network Requests

The App makes network requests to the following third-party services:

  • CoinGecko API (api.coingecko.com) — to fetch current Bitcoin and Gold prices. No personal data is transmitted. CoinGecko may log IP addresses per their own privacy policy.
  • CDN services (unpkg.com, cdn.tailwindcss.com) — to load JavaScript libraries (React, Babel, Tailwind CSS). These services may log IP addresses per their own policies.
  • Google Play Billing — for in-app purchases. Purchase data is processed by Google per the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage

All data processing happens locally on your device. The App does not store data on external servers. Purchase status is managed by Google Play.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any advertising networks, analytics platforms, social media SDKs, or crash reporting tools.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Financial Disclaimer

The App provides mathematical model-based projections and is not financial advice. All information is for educational and informational purposes only. The developers are not licensed financial advisors. Investment decisions are your sole responsibility.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the "Last updated" date at the top.

9. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
